About us


We exist to create and deliver innovative and sustainable solutions for our clients.


Angel C Holdings prides itself in having knowledge of a broad spectrum of infrastructure and management delivery in a multi-disciplinary consulting environment.


Our pursuit of excellence is driven by delivering viable and sustainable projects using our combined knowledge, skills, and expertise. Our unique approach is centered around engineering business solutions for our public and private sector clients.
